ARIMA Model

Autoregressive Integrated Moving Average model for time series forecasting.

  • Suitable for stationary or differenced stationary data
  • Captures linear relationships in time series
  • Parameters: (p,d,q) for AR, differencing, and MA orders

GARCH Model

Generalized Autoregressive Conditional Heteroskedasticity for volatility modeling.

  • Models time-varying volatility
  • Captures volatility clustering
  • Suitable for financial returns data

EWMA Model

Exponentially Weighted Moving Average for adaptive forecasting.

  • Gives more weight to recent observations
  • Simple but effective for volatility estimation
  • Single parameter (λ) controls decay rate
